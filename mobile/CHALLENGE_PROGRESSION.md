## üìà Scaling Summary

### Duration Scaling
- **Early (1-30)**: 5-20s challenges
- **Mid (31-60)**: 20-45s challenges
- **Advanced (61-90)**: 45-90s challenges
- **Mastery (91-100)**: 60-120s challenges

### Difficulty Scaling
- **Tolerance**: 30px ‚Üí 5px (gets stricter)
- **Speed**: 0.3x ‚Üí 2.0x (gets faster)
- **Item Count**: 1 ‚Üí 10+ items (memory)
- **Object Count**: 1 ‚Üí 5+ objects (tracking)
- **Distractions**: 1 ‚Üí 30+ (resistance)

### XP Scaling
- **Base**: 10 XP per challenge
- **Multiplier**: 1.0x ‚Üí 3.0x based on level
- **Perfect Bonus**: +10 XP
- **Test Bonus**: 5x XP for tests
