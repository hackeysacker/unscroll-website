# FocusFlow User Guide

## Getting Started

### 1. Welcome & Onboarding

When you first open FocusFlow, you'll go through a quick onboarding:

1. **Welcome Screen**: Learn about FocusFlow's features
2. **Goal Selection**: Choose your primary goal:
   - Reduce Doomscrolling
   - Improve Focus
   - Build Impulse Control
   - Calm Mind
3. **Baseline Test** (Optional): A 30-second focus test to determine your starting level
   - Hold the blue dot for as long as possible
   - Ignore distractions that appear
   - Your score determines your starting level (1-5)
   - You can skip this and start at Level 1

### 2. Dashboard Overview

After onboarding, you'll see your main dashboard with:

#### Stats Cards
- **Streak**: Your current daily streak (days in a row)
- **Level**: Your current level (1-30) and bracket (Beginner/Intermediate/Advanced)
- **XP**: Your current XP and progress to next level

#### Daily Training
- Shows your progress through today's 3 challenges
- Click "Start Challenge" to begin training
- Session completes after finishing 3 challenges

#### Quick Actions
- **Skill Tree**: View your progress across 3 skill paths
- **Insights**: See detailed stats and analytics
- **Settings**: Customize your app experience
- **Premium**: Upgrade to unlock additional features

## Playing Challenges

### Challenge Selection

1. Click "Start Challenge" from the dashboard
2. Choose from available challenges (unlocked based on your level)
3. Or click "Random Challenge" for a surprise

### Challenge Types

#### Gaze Hold
- **Objective**: Hold your focus on the dot
- **How to play**: Click and hold the blue dot for the duration
- **Scoring**: Based on how long you maintain focus
- **Duration**: 20-30 seconds

#### Other Challenges
All challenge types follow similar patterns:
- Clear objective
- Timed duration
- Score based on accuracy
- XP rewards on completion

### Scoring
- **0-59**: Needs improvement
- **60-79**: Good performance
- **80-94**: Great performance
- **95-100**: Perfect! (Bonus XP awarded)

## Progression System

### XP & Leveling

**Earning XP:**
- 10 XP per challenge
- 30 XP bonus per completed session (3 challenges)
- +10 XP bonus for perfect performance (95%+ score)
- Streak multiplier (after day 4)

**Level Requirements:**
- 200 XP needed per level
- 30 levels total (1-30)
- Higher levels unlock more challenges

**Level Brackets:**
- **Beginner** (1-10): Basic challenges
- **Intermediate** (11-20): More challenge types
- **Advanced** (21-30): All challenges unlocked

### Streak System

**How it works:**
- Complete a session each day to maintain your streak
- Streak increments by +1 for each consecutive day
- **Streak Freeze**: Miss 1 day and your streak is preserved (one-time use)
- Miss 2+ days and your streak resets to 0

**Streak Benefits:**
- After day 4: +10% XP multiplier
- After day 5: +20% XP multiplier
- After day 6: +30% XP multiplier
- And so on...

### Skill Trees

Three paths to master:

1. **Focus Path** (Blue)
   - Gaze Hold challenges
   - Breath Pacing challenges
   - Tracking challenges

2. **Impulse Control Path** (Purple)
   - Impulse Delay challenges
   - Tap Pattern challenges

3. **Distraction Resistance Path** (Green)
   - Distraction Resistance challenges
   - Audio Focus challenges

Each challenge you complete contributes to the relevant skill path. Aim for 100% in each!

## Tips for Success

1. **Be Consistent**: Train at the same time each day
2. **Use Streaks**: The multiplier significantly boosts your XP
3. **Focus on Accuracy**: Perfect scores (95%+) give bonus XP
4. **Balance Skill Paths**: Work on all three paths for well-rounded improvement
5. **Start Small**: Don't worry about perfect scores early on
6. **Track Progress**: Check Insights regularly to see improvement

## Troubleshooting

### My streak didn't increment
- Make sure you complete all 3 challenges in a session
- Sessions must be completed on different calendar days

### I can't access certain challenges
- Challenges unlock at specific levels
- Keep leveling up to unlock more challenge types

### How do I reset my progress?
- Go to Settings > Account > Reset Progress
- Confirm the action (this cannot be undone)

### Dark mode isn't working
- Go to Settings > App Settings
- Toggle "Dark Mode" on/off
- The change should apply immediately
